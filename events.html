"Study to shew thyself approved unto god, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth." 2 Timothy 2:15

The arrival of the month of August calls an end to the "lazy days of summer" and the beginning of a brand new school year. A new school year means new classes, new subjects, new ideas and new discoveries as well as new challenges. Normally, in school you are given tests to gage your knowledge of the subject matter that has been taught. In order to successfully pass the test, you must study! Studying reinforces the information that has been given to you.

Our own daily lives are full of tests and the only way we can pass them is to read and study our life's textbook, The Word of God! We have to study and know the Word of God so much so until it becomes a part of who we are. The Word of God will bring revelation and light to your path. Open up your bible, read and study it, so when the pop quizzes of life appear, you will be ready to stand on the word and having done all, stand!
