Welcome to the New Beginnings Ministries of Peoria, home on the Web. This is the start of your "New Beginnings". We are a ministry that has been called by the Lord, Jesus Christ to build a people of purpose, power and praise! We depend on the Holy Spirit to lead us exactly where God wants us to go. We know that an understanding of God's Word is what you need to change your life. At New Beginnings Ministries of Peoria, we're out to reach lives for the Kingdom of God, promoting a positive life and salvation in Jesus Christ. We are touching the broken, hurting, and desperate lives with the love of God and the understanding that Christ came to give them life and that more abundantly. Take a moment to explore our church home on the web and be forever blessed! Remember that you're always welcome at New Beginnings Ministries of Peoria.
