our mission is to prepare the youth, naturally and spiritually, to be effective in the kingdom of god. to support our youth in their individual walks with christ in whatever way the lord allows. to equip them to be able to draw others to christ by being followers of christ's example. to ultimately see them all saved and filled with the holy spirit.

at new beginnings ministries of peoria, we encourage our youth to be kingdom builders by allowing them the opportunity to participate in various ministries and outreach efforts. some of our regular activities include:
  • special field trips
  • angelic praise dancers
  • victorious praise dancers
  • faith steppers step team
  • life skills bible discussions
  • youth night group outings
  • children's choir
  • daughters of destiny young ladies group
