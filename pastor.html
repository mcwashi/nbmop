Elder Martin Johnson is the founder and pastor of New Beginnings Ministries of Peoria. a ministry designed to build a people of purpose, power and praise. He and his lovely wife, Lady Lisa serve together as faithful servants in the kingdom of god.

He is a dedicated disciple and minister of Jesus Christ and for nearly twenty years he has been ministering the gospel of Jesus Christ and attending to the needs of souls. He serves with valor, authority, insight, caring, and the affectionate touch of a good shepherd.

His ministry has been largely birthed on love and compassion in help making a difference in the lives of people. His practical preaching and teaching has allowed him to bless the body of christ at large. In fulfillment of the great commission, the Lord has favored him with a daily radio broadcast, "Your New Beginnings".

Pastor Johnson is an alumnus of Aenon Bible College, Moody Bible College, and Liberty University. He has also been serving honorably for the last twenty six years in the United States Air Force.

In addition to being married to his one and only, Lady Lisa, Eld. Johnson cherishes his three children, Tavis, Martez and Tia Johnson.
